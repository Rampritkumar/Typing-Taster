Pressing Keys

Each key is pressed by the finger on the home row that is closest. After reaching a key away from the home row, the finger needs to return to its home row key.

Example: How to Type Letter F and J

  • Make sure that your fingers are on their home row keys.
  • Move your left index finger from fupwards to r and right index
    finger from J upwards to U. Your hand may move slightly to
    make it easier to reach F and J.
  • Press F and U with a quick and light, touch keeping your hand relaxed.
  • Move the index finger back to its home key F and J.
